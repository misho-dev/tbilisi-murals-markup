David Samkharadze

David Samkharadze was born in tbilisi in 1985. started doing oilpaintings from 1998 with georgian old generation artist Temo Machavariani, same time studing sculpture in art college named I. Nikoladze. in 2008 finished studing Arcitecture in tbilisi academy of arts. in past years worked as architector and doing different styles of arts as graphic, mural and oilpaintings. currently working on muralpaintings and architecture.
