Luis Gomez De Teran

He created artworks in many countries of the world, in Italy, England, Germany, Spain, India, Tunis, Thailand, and little towns, where public art is still able to have a deep social impact on the local people. Gomez took part in several collective exhibitions throughout Europe and in June 2016, he had his solo show Nox Omnibus Lucet at the gallery Varsi in Rome. It was then followed by a personal exhibition in Bruxelles with Montoro12 gallery, a second one in Rome with Philobiblon gallery and his first hybrid painting and sculpting exhibition in 2019 at MUSMA, the museum of contemporary sculpture in Matera.
