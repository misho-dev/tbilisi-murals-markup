Noah Kauertz & Aphe

The artists APHE (Oliver Hollatz) and NOAH (Noah Kauertz) got to know each other during their art studies in Bonn. Their common interest in art in public space and their passion for painting led to the first mural collaboration in 2019. In the same year, four more murals were created, which significantly shaped the collaboration between the artists in terms of color, style and subject. Their dialogic painting moves fluidly between representation and abstraction and is strongly influenced by an atmospheric color aesthetics.
