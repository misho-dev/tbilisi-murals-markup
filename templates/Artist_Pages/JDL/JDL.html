JDL

JDL Street art aka Judith de Leeuw started aerosol cans at the age of 15 as a rebel on the street. The multiple award winning artist was born and raised in Amsterdam, Netherlands. In 2015 she graduated in Art & Design which was followed by a one-year bachelor at the Willem de Kooning Academy in 2016. Her art evolved quickly, as she became an established artist in a very short period. Currently JDL travels worldwide as an international artist to realise her large-scale murals.
