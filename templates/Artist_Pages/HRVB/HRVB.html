Hi. I'm Tom, a freelance illustrator and graffiti artist located in Berlin. My focus is on character design, complex illustrations and painting murals. The projects I work for are insanely diverse and consequently the challenge that is needed for an artist to develop. I'm an inherently curious person and absorbing my environment with eyes and ears. Furthermore, I'm a really fun loving person and always up for anything. In any way, my brain seems to link all these information and emotions and to reissue them in form of my paintings. This is what constitutes my inspiration in the creative process.
