Faith47

Faith47 / Faith XLVII (born Cape Town, 1979) is a South African interdisciplinary artist who has held solo exhibitions in Miami (2018), New York City (2015), London (2014) and Johannesburg (2012). As both a notable South African and woman artist, Faith speaks to issues of human rights, spiritual endurance and social issues.
