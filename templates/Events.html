Workshop

On the 26th of September, Co-founders of the Tbilisi Art Festival are organizing a workshop, in association with the Tbilisi Art Academy. The one-day workshop, lead by top european artists, will be accessible to all students of the Tbilisi Art Acadedemy and anyone interested in street art.
