Workshop

On the 26th of September, Co-founders of the Tbilisi Art Festival are organizing a workshop, in association with the Tbilisi Art Academy. The one-day workshop, lead by top european artists, will be accessible to all students of the Tbilisi Art Acadedemy and anyone interested in street art. The Tbilisi Mural Festival, the first of it's kind in Georgia, organized by the city halls of Berlin and Tbilisi, will take place from the 17th of september, all through the 25th. During the festival, world-famous street artists will paint 10 murals in the streets of Tbilisi.
